Cool Earth

Cool Earth is a UK-based international NGO which strives to protect endangered rainforest in order to fight against global warming, protect the local ecosystems and to provide employment for local people.

In 2015, it was named Charity of the Year in its category at the Civil Society Media Charity Awards and best International NGO at the PEA Awards.

For more information, visit their website:
coolearth.org

LauraLynn Children's Hospice

As a team, we decided to fundraise for the LauraLynn Children's Hospice. LauraLynn is a hospice for children with life-limiting conditions and residential care for young adults with disabilities.

It is the only children's hospice in Ireland and was set up in 2001. They aim to offer care for the child as well as support and information for families. LauraLynn believes in caring for children with life-limiting conditions by providing respite, transitional, crisis, home support, and end-of-life care.

For more information, visit their website:
lauralynn.ie
